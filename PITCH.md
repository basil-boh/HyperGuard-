# 🛡️ HyperGuard — Spoken Pitch

**This is HyperGuard — an agentic payment protection layer.**

It stops scams in real time: it scores every bank transfer, calls the customer to
verify the suspicious ones, blocks the fraud, and alerts their family — before they
send their savings to a scammer.

### Why it matters

Modern scams don't breach the bank — they breach the person. The victim is talked into
authorising the payment themselves, so every traditional fraud check waves it straight
through. **94% of fraud losses are payments people send with their own hands** — and
the whole scam lives in the sixty seconds between *"I'll send it"* and *"sent."*

### How it works

Five agents act inside that window. A **Digital Twin** scores every transfer against
the customer's normal; when risk spikes, a **Voice Negotiator** calls them, an
**Educator** recognises the scam script live and warns them, and a **Guardian** loops
in their family — so the money never leaves the account. If fraud ever slips through, a
**Recovery Coordinator** builds the evidence pack for the bank and police.

### The control centre

The bank watches it all from mission control — live risk scores, call transcripts and
verdicts, every case filed and every decision explainable and audited.

### Close

**Detection tells you *after*. HyperGuard intervenes *during*.** We put an AI agent in
the room the moment someone's about to be robbed.
